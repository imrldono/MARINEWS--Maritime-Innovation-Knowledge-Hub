Our Strategic Goals

Driving innovation and collaboration to transform research into real-world applications and solutions

  • 1
    Knowledge Transfer:

    Bridges the gap between scientific research and industry application, moving ideas from concept to market.

  • 2
    Collaboration:

    Creates networks for academics, businesses, and policymakers to work together.

  • 3
    Training & Education:

    Offers specialized education and simulations (e.g., ship-handling) to prepare the future workforce.

  • 4
    Regulatory Support:

    Helps innovators navigate complex regulations (like the UK's) to speed up new technology deployment.

  • 5
    Innovation Focus:

    Drives development in key areas such as smart shipping, alternative fuels, digital systems, and autonomous vessels.

These goals guide our mission to accelerate technological advancement and foster sustainable innovation.
